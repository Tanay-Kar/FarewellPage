How we are here

The one because of whom we are where we are is ...

Our Great mother

Ms Deblina Maity

Teaching

Deblina Ma'am had an amazing teaching capability. She had the unspoken skill to explain the most complex of topics in the most lucid manner. She never believed in a locked classroom and rather explained the topics in ways that we could relate to in class. Having her as our teacher was a blessing.

Understanding

We were blessed with a teacher on whom we could depend. She was not only a class teacher but also a friend, a mentor, an altruist, a supporter and a guide. She was always there for us and we could always count on her. She was the one who understood us the most and was the guiding light in all of a our endevours.

Encouragement

She has been an incredible source of encouragement for us. She has always motivated us to do better and better. Her in-lecture anecdotes about the reality of life and the importance of being a good human being will be forever etched in our hearts.

Behaviour

As mentioned before , Deblina ma'am was rather a mother for us. She was more than a teacher, she was a mentor, a parent and above all she was like a huge tree whose huge branches provided a shelter and comfort to us all. She was also a sun radiating the warmth and light of knowledge to us all. She was and will always remain our great mother.
